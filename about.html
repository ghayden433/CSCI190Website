Welcome to StrideTime, your go-to platform for analyzing running results from top sources like MileSplit, Athletic.net, World Athletics, and NCAA. Powered by Python, our platform leverages cutting-edge web scraping techniques to collect, organize, and present comprehensive data on track and field performances, cross-country results, and road races.

Whether you’re a coach, athlete, or fan, StrideTime simplifies access to the stats you need, offering insights into trends, rankings, and individual performance progressions. Stay ahead of the competition with accurate, real-time data—because every second counts.
